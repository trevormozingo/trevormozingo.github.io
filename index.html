Seattle Recommendations

Parks/Scenery/Hiking:

Gas Works Park

Grassy, hilly park on the north side of Lake Union. With beautiful views of the city, it's perfect for a picnic.

Alki Beach

Good restaurants, bars, and an amazing view of the city. It's also great for beach activities.

Golden Gardens Park

Another good beach with more beach activities and better sand than Alki. It's perfect for BBQs and sports.

Green Lake

A perfect area for hiking with trails and nearby sports courts for tennis, soccer, and skateboarding.

Cal Anderson Park

A park on Capitol Hill with playing fields. Many people do recreational sports here, such as soccer, volleyball, and baseball.

Day/Weekend Places (Further away):

Whidbey Island

Langley

Another touristy beach town on Whidbey Island. It offers lots of arts like painting and glasswork, and a few bars and restaurants.

Deception Pass

A beautiful park with hiking and camping opportunities, offering incredible views.

Leavenworth

A fun touristy town made to look like a Bavarian German town up in the Cascades.

Skagit Valley Tulip Festival

Seabrook (2hr drive)

A beautiful beach town along the Pacific.

Fun Towns/Neighborhoods:

Edmonds

Kind of a touristy beach town vibe. Pretty laid back.

Ballard

Good restaurants and bars. Known for its Scandinavian heritage.

Fremont

Eclectic and artsy neighborhood with interesting shops, restaurants, and a troll sculpture.

Capitol Hill

Trendy and vibrant neighborhood with a lively nightlife scene, diverse restaurants, and shops.

Queen Anne

Residential neighborhood with beautiful views of the city from Kerry Park.
